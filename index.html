Study Style

Visual learners learn best when information is presented in a written language format or in another visual format such as pictures or diagrams. If you are a visual learner, the suggestions that follow can help you to succeed in school to the best of your ability. Create graphic organizers such as diagrams and concept maps that use visual symbols to represent ideas and information. When trying to remember information, close your eyes and visualize the information. Include illustrations as you take notes in class. Use highlighter pens of contrasting colors to color code different aspects of the information in your textbooks. Sit in the front of the class so that you can clearly see the teacher. This will allow you to pick up facial expressions and body language that provide cues that what your teacher is saying is important to write in your notes.

Study in a place that is free from visual distractions. When using flashcards, limit the amount of information on a card so that you can form a mental picture of the information. Watch videos about topics you are studying in class. When hearing a new word you want to remember, visualize its spelling. When reviewing information, rewrite or draw the information from memory. When taking notes, replace words with symbols wherever possible. Type your written notes from class using different fonts, bold print, and underlining to make the most important concepts and facts visually apparent. When solving math problems that involve a sequence of steps, draw a series of boxes, each containing the appropriate piece of information in sequence.

Since the appearance of smart phone, people's life is becoming more and more convenient and simple. My thoughts also start from life .I remember before that it was popular to use cell phones to control air conditioners in summer. This means that the mobile phone could replace the air conditioner remote control. Otherwise, there are still many appliances in our lives that require specific remote controls or switches. I hope to develop a mobile phone software as the control center of all household appliances. The systems and networks of these appliances are tied directly to the phone. They receive commands from the phone and change their status accordingly.

In fact, everyone's home is full of light switches or remote controls. They require a certain amount of production cost and some of them require the use of batteries. We could avoid this waste entirely. Now some lights have inductive switches and it turns off automatically after a while. For this, our mobile phone could also achieve it through timing. Most importantly, it could replace all the control buttons. People don't need to look for specific switches or remote controls to give commands. Some appliances that need to be used at fixed times could also be automated by using the phone to set the time. In addition, people have such control even when they are not at home. More details will be explained in the next section.

The control of this software receives instructions in two ways. The first is that we can normally use the phone touch the corresponding options. The second is artificial intelligence. Brands of smartphones now have their own artificial intelligence, which uses voice recognition to find software, information or run commands directly. In this process, we just need to say the name of the appliance we want to control and the action we want to achieve to the mobile phone. This progress makes it easier and more direct for people to control home appliances. In terms of security, I just assume that the phone can only recognize the voice of the user.

Of course, the phone's fingerprint unlock and password is also a layer of protection. As for the setting of time, we can set a fixed switch period for each electrical appliance according to everyone's habit. This is similar to how we set our alarm clocks. The corresponding appliance will run automatically when it reaches the set time. The initial interface displays the switching state of all appliances. It is convenient for people to check the safety of home appliances because some appliances can damage themselves or waste energy if left on for a long time. On the other hand, each appliance is fitted with a dedicated scanning device that feeds back real-time state information. This is just my assumption because it's very difficult to achieve either cost or technical universality.

For example, our TV sometimes fails to play or goes dark.The corresponding problem will be detected and immediately sent to the phone. Solutions to the problem are also listed. If it's a regular problem we can get the phone to fix itself. So people could set up their refrigerators to work anywhere, anytime. A miniature robot sweeping the floor at home can also be scanned regularly to see if there is a safety hazard. Each appliance provides the user with actual information about themselves or their home. Some lights are equipped with heat-sensing features to check if there are strangers or even small animals in the house.
